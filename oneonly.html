Overview

One&Only is a platform to help those girls who want to make their unique wedding dress. You can find a lot of tutorials here, you can also see other people's cases and wedding dress inspirations, you can even buy the raw materials you need here, and rent the professional equipment you need. There is also a question and answer board where you can get your questions answered by professional designers. One&Only hopes to solve all kinds of problems faced by DIY wedding dresses.

Research

To validate the quality of the idea, I conducted some quick user surveys with the primary audience that I was targeting. I surveyed 23 girls and conducted 6 semi-structured interviews to understand what problems girls are facing first-hand and what they care about the most.

Takeaways

This case study is created for a small group of audiences. At present, I have not found any similar app or product in the market. As an app with a new concept, there is no other competitor to be analyzed and no enough data support, which requires more surveys, interviews, and testing. Next, I will collect more data, do more testing, continue to improve this app, and strive to give the users the best experience.
