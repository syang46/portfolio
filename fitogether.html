Overview

Fitogether is a tool and platform that allows you to start exercising anywhere and anytime. You can customize your plans, work out with professional trainers, and share your exercise experience with your friends.

Problems

For some people, it's difficult to find time to go to a gym or it might be too much for them to pay for gym member fees for those facilities they donâ€™t need.
Now during the COVID-19 pandemic, people who used to go to the gym can't go anymore. Exercising at home is the best option for them, however, without the company of friends and the guidance of a professional coach, itâ€™s very easy to lose motivation and give up.

My Goal

Make an application that allows people to get guidance from professional trainers while working out with friends without limitation of venue and time.

Research

I have conducted an online survey with 32 potential users and done 3 one-on-one interviews to learn their opinions and experience, and collected both qualitative and quantitative data.

Testing

I've conducted two rounds of user testing with 4 participants in each round. I shared my low-fidelity wireframes with them and had them complete the three tasks. I then recorded their comments and went back to my wireframes to make changes.

Based on the feedback I received, I created medium-fidelity wireframes and presented the participants in the second round of testing. I then analyzed their feedback and iterate the design quickly.

Takeaways

From this project, I learned that it may not be difficult to find the pain points of users, but how to effectively solve problems is challenging. User testing and iteration are the keys to improve the design. Even if our job is to design for everyday life, personally or professionally, that doesn't mean we have an excuse to create something boring. Instead, we should observe our life more to inspirations, create more useful and interesting products.
